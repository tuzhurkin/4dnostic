Blog

Our press service is open and transparent for any media representatives. We are always ready to provide you with up-to-date, complete and reliable information about the clinic's activities. You can also get an expert opinion of our doctors, comments, analytical information on early diagnosis of diseases. We value your time and are very attentive to every question from the media and opinion leaders. Contact us at

Blog

We write articles so that as many people as possible can see the benefits of early diagnosis and be able to prevent serious diseases or eliminate them in their early stages.

Well-informed patients consciously come to us for a complete body checkup. We're much more likely to help them by keeping track of their health, coming in earlier and more often, and understanding why they need to follow certain recommendations. That's how we save more lives.
